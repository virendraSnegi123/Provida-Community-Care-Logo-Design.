Mission

› Self-dependent of children with autism & multiple disability.

› Educating & training special educators for autism & multiple disabilities.

› Awareness, counseling & supporting to the parents/families of autism & multiple disability children.

› Help & participate in autism & multiple disability research programme.

Vision

› Holistic development & provide equal opportunities to autistic & children with special needs.

Who We Are

› Welcome to Autism Welfare Society “a Home for Autistic & Multiple Disability Children”. The Autism Welfare Society (AWS) ‘A Home for Autistic & Multiple Disability Children’ is a non-profit charitable society founded by a group of people who have Passion, Love & Burden for the autistic & multiple disability children in the foothills of Himalayan Mountain range the place call “Dehradun” Uttarakhand with an objective of Holistic Development of individual.
